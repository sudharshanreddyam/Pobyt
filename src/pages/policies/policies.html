Guest Policies

GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

  • The primary guest must be at least 18 years of age to be able to check-in the hotel.
  • It is mandatory for guests to present valid photo identification at the time of check-in. According to government regulations, a valid Photo ID has to be carried by every person above the age of 18 staying at the hotel. The identification proofs accepted are Adhar Card, Driving License, Voter ID Card, and Passport. Without valid ID the guest will not be allowed to check-in.
  • PAN cards are not accepted as a valid ID card.
  • Stay of 1 child up to 5 years of age is complementary without the use of extra mattress.
  • Pets are not allowed in the hotel premises.
  • Certain destinations may have different policies for specific times during the year.
  • Guests shall be liable for any damage, except normal wear and tear to Hotel asset. Guest shall keep the Hotel room in a good condition and maintain hygiene and cleanliness.
